Text: Show houses are an industry fundraiser designed to garner top designers exposure while also helping a worthy not for profit raise funds. The vetting process is extremely competitive so designers have to be on top of their game. Favreau Design was chosen to create two spaces for a well-travelled family with a diverse art collection and love for a creative mix of furnishings, color and texture.
